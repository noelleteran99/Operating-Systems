Windows 10 has improved functions from the last version of Windows 8.
Improvements were made to have the Start Menu return with easy access.
Multitasking has been made easier for users.  And the Xbox app can now be 
installed on your laptop or desktop and stream games to play.

functions.  Some of those improvements are Siri now has enhanced intelligence and
